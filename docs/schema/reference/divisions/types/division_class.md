# DivisionClass

Further classification of a division that is more specific than its `subtype`.

A division's `class` adds detail to the broad classification found in `DivisionSubtype`.

## Values

- `megacity` - A very large city or metropolitan area, typically having a population of 10 million or
more. Example: Tokyo, Japan.
- `city` - A large, permanent human settlement. Example: Guadalajara, Mexico.
- `town` - A medium-sized permanent human settlement that is smaller than a city, but larger than a
village. Example: Walldürn, Germany.
- `village` - A smallish permanent human settlement that is smaller than a town, but larger than a
hamlet. Example: Wadi El Karm, Lebanon.
- `hamlet` - A very small, isolated human settlement in a rural area. Example: Tjarnabyggð, Iceland.
