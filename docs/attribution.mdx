title: Attribution and Licensing
---

If you are the author of a publication or research report that uses Overture data, you may choose to add the following citation, including the date the data were accessed: `Overture Maps Foundation, overturemaps.org`. 

The Overture Maps Foundation does not require text attribution or an OMF logo on maps, visualizations, and graphics created with our datasets. If you would like to credit Overture, we suggest: `© Overture Maps Foundation`. Some of the data sources we use in Overture datasets require their own attribution, according to their licenses. See below for more information.

### Addresses
**License for theme:** The address data come from a variety of sources. All carry permissive open licenses. Some have special terms or require attribution. These terms are outlined below.

Distributed by OpenAddresses. Accessed: 2024-07-07.
- Austria: © [Austrian Address Register](https://geometadatensuche.inspire.gv.at/metadatensuche/srv/ger/catalog.search#/metadata/bd28c2b0-4ea5-41c2-8847-b7e8711a6512), data accessed 10 July 2024; available under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/). Distributed by OpenAddresses. Accessed: 2024-07-07.
- Canada: [Statistics Canada, National Address Register](https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/n1/pub/46-26-0002/462600022022001-eng.htm), 2024. Reproduced and distributed on an "as is" basis with the permission of Statistics Canada; available under [OGL - Statistics Canada Open Licence](https://www.statcan.gc.ca/en/reference/licence). - Luxembourg: [The Luxembourg open data platform](https://data.public.lu/fr/); available under [CC0](https://creativecommons.org/public-domain/cc0/). Distributed by OpenAddresses. Accessed: 2024-07-07.
- Mexico: [INEGI, Censos Económicos](https://en.www.inegi.org.mx/default.html); [TÉRMINOS DE LIBRE USO DE LA INFORMACIÓN DEL INEGI](https://www.inegi.org.mx/contenidos/inegi/doc/terminos_info.pdf). Distributed by OpenAddresses. Accessed: 2024-07-07.

- Portugal: [Instituto Nacional de Estatística](https://www.ine.pt/xportal/xmain?xpgid=ine_main&xpid=INE). Distributed by OpenAddresses. Accessed: 2024-07-07.
